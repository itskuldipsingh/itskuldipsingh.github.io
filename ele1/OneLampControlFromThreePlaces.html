Aim

To wire up PVC conduit wiring to control one lamp from three different places using two 2-way switches and one intermediate switch.

Requirements

Insulated Screwdriver
Neon Tester
PVC Insulated Cable

Demonstration

Installation Procedure:
  1. Connect the Phase wire to the common terminal of Switch 1 (S1).
  2. Connect travelers from S1 to the first set of terminals on the Intermediate Switch (S2).
  3. Connect the second set of terminals on S2 to the travelers of Switch 3 (S3).
  4. Connect the common terminal of S3 to the Lamp holder.
  5. Connect the Neutral wire directly to the lamp holder to complete the circuit.

Safety & Precautions

  • Always switch off the main supply and remove the fuse before commencing any wiring work to prevent electric shock.
  • Ensure that the Phase wire is always connected to the switches and never directly to the lamp.
  • Check the continuity of the intermediate switch in both positions using a multimeter before installation.
  • Ensure all screw connections are tight; loose connections can lead to overheating and potential fires.
  • Keep an extra 200mm to 300mm of cable length at each termination point for comfortable wiring.
